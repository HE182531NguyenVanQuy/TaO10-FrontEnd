PHẦN I – Phát âm (Câu 1–2)
Mark the word whose underlined part differs from the other three in pronunciation.

2 câu
1
Question 1. Choose the word whose underlined part is pronounced differently from the other three:
A
endangered
B
generation
C
embroider
D
environment
✅ Đáp án đúng: B – generation
Các từ A, C, D có âm /ɪ/ (endangered, embroider, environment). Riêng generation có âm /e/ ở âm tiết "ge-". Đáp án là B.
2
Question 2. Choose the word whose underlined part is pronounced differently from the other three:
A
handicraft
B
communicate
C
remind
D
traditional
✅ Đáp án đúng: C – remind
Riêng remind phát âm /aɪ/. Các từ A, B, D phát âm /ɪ/. Đáp án là C.

PHẦN III – Tìm lỗi sai (Câu 5–8)
Mark the underlined part that needs correction.

4 câu
5
Question 5. When the church was first establish in 1973, it consisted of only two hectares of land.
A
was
B
establish
C
consisted
D
land
✅ Đáp án đúng: B – establish → established
Câu dùng thể bị động, sau "was first" cần V3 (established).
6
Question 6. What do you practice speaking English all day for? Winning a prize.
A
what
B
speaking
C
for
D
winning
✅ Đáp án đúng: D – winning → to win
Mục đích trả lời "What...for?" dùng to + V.
7
Question 7. The Pikes put away their trip because of bad weather.
A
The
B
put away
C
because of
D
weather
✅ Đáp án đúng: B – put away → put off
"Put away" = cất đi. Đúng nghĩa phải là "put off" (hoãn lại).
8
Question 8. The children were so amusing at the cartoon that they couldn't help laughing.
A
so
B
amusing
C
that
D
couldn't help laughing
✅ Đáp án đúng: B – amusing → amused
Cảm xúc của người dùng V3 (amused).

used to go

PHẦN VIII – Đọc hiểu đoạn văn (Câu 31–34)
Read the passage and indicate the correct answer.

4 câu

Every year people in many countries learn English. Some of them are young children. Others are teenagers. Many are adults. Some learn at school. Others study by themselves. A few learn English just by hearing the language in films, on television, in the office or among their friends. Most people must work hard to learn English.

Why do all these people learn English? It's not difficult to answer this question. Many boys and girls learn English at school because it is one of their subjects. Many adults learn English because it is useful for their work. Teenagers often learn English for their higher studies because some of their books are in English at the college or university. Other people learn English because they want to read newspapers or magazines in English.

31
Question 31. According to the passage, who wants to learn English?
A
young children
B
teenagers
C
adults
D
All are correct.
✅ D – All are correct.
Đoạn văn đề cập cả trẻ em, thanh thiếu niên và người lớn.
32
Question 32. In the second part, the word "it" refers to ____________.
A
country
B
young children
C
English
D
question
✅ C – English
"it" trong "it is useful for their work" chỉ về English.
33
Question 33. According to the passage, in what way isn't English learnt?
A
by hearing the language in films
B
on television
C
in the office
D
in newspapers
✅ D – in newspapers
Đoạn văn không đề cập học qua newspapers.
34
Question 34. Which sentence is INCORRECT?
A
Most people must work hard to learn English.
B
English is not compulsory at school.
C
Some of the books are in English at the college or university.
D
It's not difficult to find the reason why these people learn English.
✅ B
Câu B sai vì bài đọc nói tiếng Anh là một môn học ở trường.
